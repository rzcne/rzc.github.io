ABOUT

BINGBOOK (hereinafter referred to as the "Company") was established on August 8, 2008 and is headquartered in Georgetown, Cayman. As an investment company focusing on the power, supply chain, banking, retail and logistics industries, we are committed to discovering and investing in high-quality projects and companies with growth potential to help industry development and value creation.

Our vision is to drive sustainable growth of our portfolio companies through strategic investments and value-added services, bringing substantial returns to our company. To achieve this goal, we have an experienced professional investment and management team with keen insight into market trends and the ability to accurately capture investment opportunities.

The company's current main areas of focus

1. Power industry: We invest in projects such as renewable energy, smart grids, energy storage technology and power infrastructure to promote energy transformation and green development.

2. Supply chain finance: We provide financing, factoring, financial leasing and other financial services to core enterprises and small and medium-sized enterprises in the supply chain, optimizing the supply chain capital flow and reducing financing costs.

3. Banking industry: We focus on investment opportunities in banks’ digital transformation, retail banking business, risk management and compliance systems, etc., to help banks improve their competitiveness and profitability.

4. Retail industry: We invest in projects such as new retail models, consumer brands and e-commerce platforms to meet the increasingly diverse needs of consumers and promote innovation and development in the retail industry.

5. Logistics and supply chain management: We focus on investment opportunities in logistics infrastructure, smart logistics, cold chain logistics and other fields to improve logistics efficiency and reduce costs.

The company will always adhere to the concept of value investment, grow together with the investment portfolio companies, and create sustained and stable returns for the company.
